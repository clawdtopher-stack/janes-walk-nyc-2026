Jane’s Walk NYC 2026

Jane’s Walk NYC 2026 is May 1-3! Join us at our annual celebration of New York City life, history, culture, advocacy, and more!

During Jane’s Walk NYC weekend, the simple act of exploring the city is enhanced with personal observations, local history, and civic engagement. Jane’s Walk NYC encourages people to share stories about their neighborhoods, discover unseen aspects of their communities, and connect with visitors and neighbors alike. Learn more about the festival >

Registration is now open! All walks are free and open to the public. Jane’s Walk NYC is rain or shine.

Questions for the Jane’s Walk NYC team? Email janeswalknyc@mas.org.

Make a gift today and show your love for Jane’s Walk NYC! Your tax-deductible gift will help us reach new audiences across the five boroughs, train and support our Jane’s Walk volunteers, and ensure that the festival is always free. Make a gift here >
